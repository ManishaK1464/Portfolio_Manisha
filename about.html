01 — Bio

I'm a graduate student in Electrical Systems Engineering at Universität Paderborn, focused on machine learning, reinforcement learning, and photonic computing. I currently research LLM-driven reinforcement learning for autonomous drone navigation at the RAT Lab, and tutor for the Electromagnetic Waves and Waveguides course under Prof. Dr. J. Förstner.

Before returning to research, I spent three years as an Azure Data Engineer at Capgemini building enterprise cloud data platforms, and eighteen months as a working student at dSPACE GmbH in software testing and analytics for automotive and aerospace embedded systems. I'm Microsoft certified in Azure Data Engineering (DP-203) and Azure Fundamentals (AZ-900).
