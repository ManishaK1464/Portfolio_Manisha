About

From data platforms to drone research

I'm a graduate student in Electrical Systems Engineering at Universität Paderborn, focused on machine learning, reinforcement learning, and photonic computing. I currently research LLM-driven reinforcement learning for autonomous drone navigation at the RAT Lab, and tutor for the Electromagnetic Waves and Waveguides course under Prof. Dr. J. Förstner.

Before returning to research, I spent three years as an Azure Data Engineer at Capgemini building enterprise cloud data platforms, and eighteen months as a working student at dSPACE GmbH in software testing and analytics for automotive and aerospace embedded systems.

LLM-guided reward generation for autonomous drone navigation.

Diffractive Deep Neural Network (D²NN)

Universität Paderborn · Oct 2025 – Mar 2026

All-optical MNIST classifier using photonic phase-mask layers.

  • Trainable phase-mask layers with Rayleigh–Sommerfeld propagation, ~79% validation accuracy.
  • Two-mask design improved accuracy by 11.7% at 1.52× training cost.
