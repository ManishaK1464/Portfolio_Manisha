Diffractive Deep Neural Network (D²NN): Photonic Machine Learning

Universität Paderborn · Oct 2025 – Mar 2026
  • Implemented an all-optical MNIST classifier in TensorFlow/Keras with trainable phase-mask layers and Rayleigh–Sommerfeld free-space propagation, reaching approximately 79% validation accuracy.
  • Benchmarked architecture variants: a two-mask design improved accuracy by 11.7% at 1.52× training cost, and a 60λ propagation distance outperformed the baseline.
